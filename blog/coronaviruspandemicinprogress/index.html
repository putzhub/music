Corona Virus Pandemic In Progress

dom 15 marzo 2020

#poetry 

CORONA VIRUS PANDEMIC IN PROGRESS:

Couldn't
Offer
Relief
Or
Nobody'd
Allow

Villianous
Ineptitude
Rampaging
Unchecked,
Sorry!

Perhaps
Another
November
Disappointing
Everyone
Might
Impede
Collapse?

It's
Not

Prudent
Relying
On
Grifters
Returning
Everyone's
Stolen
Shit.
