My New Synth

lun 12 julio 2021

#music

Hello everybody! I recently got a new synthesizer. It's a Korg Nautilus if you care to know. Compare it to the CTK-720 (my old keyboard) and you'll see what that means haha. Most of you who probably know me have already seen the results of that on instagram anyhow.

This is just for those of you finding this space who might not otherwise know yet.

Anyway, long story short I'll be putting up a lot more music. I'll post anything big like an album release here, but if you wanna see the magic happen in real time over the days then you can follow my Instagram or my Twitter (which is the same as the instagram anyway lol).

That's it for now, expect more to come.
